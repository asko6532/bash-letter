dear bash...........

I have to tell you - I love you. Your touch creates something new and special. You help me branch out and explore new things. It might seem like I’m always commanding you to do things, but it’s only because I love you. You gave me an iPod nano for christmas and it opened my eyes to something new. I love how you make everything so clear and simple.

The way you work is simple. Use pwd to find out what directory you’re in. Type li to find out where you can go. Use cd to go forward and cd- to go back. The touch command creates and new file and the nano command opens up a text editor. When you want to make a new folder (directory) you simply say mkdir. You are full of shortcuts. Ctrl+a moves you to the beginning of a line, and ctrl+b moves you back one letter. Ctrl+c halts the current command. I’m so glad I met you, and I think I’ll love you forever.

Love,

Ashley
